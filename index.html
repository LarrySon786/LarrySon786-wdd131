Ohio, United States

Ohio is the 7th largest state by population in the country. It is home to lots of industry, agriculture, and transportation centers. It is famous for being the birthplace of aviation as the Wright brothers invented the first plane here at their home. Ohio also is tied with Virgina for having the most US presidents. Ohio's history is rich and has played a big part in the nation's development. From civil war times, to American music, Ohio has left a significant footprint on the country.
